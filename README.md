## Controls
- World Rotate - click and drag Mouse Left Button (`MLB`)
- World Zoom - Mouse Wheel

## Actions
+ 🐔 Dwellers Move - click and drag `MLB`
+ 🌿 Feed animals - click `MLB` on Plant and drag to Animal
+ 💰 Sell goods - click to icons on top menu
